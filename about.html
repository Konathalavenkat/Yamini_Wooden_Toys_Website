About Us

yamini wooden toys manufacturers is the most leading wooden lacquer toys in the village Etikoppaka.The main motive of yamini wooden toys is to promote women empowerment. Earlier only men used to make this toys work, we could hardly encounter women making this work. But now the number of women working in this field is almost equal to the men. this achievement is made by mr chinnayachari.

Mr.Chinnayachari the founder of Yamini wooden toys has got the national award from the president of india for his fabulus artwork in this wooden handicraft. And the most important thing is he gives training to the new workers. He has created a nice platform for this handicraft by providing internships and job opportunities to the people interested in this handicraft.

In this factory a wide variety of toys are prepared suitable for all types of occasions.Customers from different regions contact this factory for the toys of their interest.
